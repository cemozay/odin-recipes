Manti

Description

Manti is a traditional Turkish dish which includes small dumplings filled with ground lamb or beef. It is commonly eaten for lunch or dinner and is served with yogurt and garlic sauce.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground lamb or beef
  • 1 cup flour
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1/4 cup salt
  • 1/4 cup pepper
  • 1/4 cup yogurt
  • 1/4 cup garlic
  • 1/4 cup parsley

Steps

  1. Mix the ground lamb or beef with salt and pepper.
  2. Roll out the dough and cut it into small squares.
  3. Fill the squares with the meat mixture and fold them into small dumplings.
  4. Boil the dumplings until cooked.
  5. Mix the yogurt and garlic together.
  6. Serve the dumplings with the yogurt and garlic sauce and sprinkle parsley on top.
