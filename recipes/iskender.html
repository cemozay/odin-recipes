Iskender Kebab

Description

Ä°skender Kebab is a traditional Turkish dish which includes thinly sliced lamb, pita bread, tomato sauce, and yogurt. It is commonly eaten for lunch or dinner and is served with rice.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb lamb, thinly sliced
  • 1 pita bread
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1/4 cup parsley
  • 1/4 cup sumac
  • 1/4 cup red pepper flakes
  • 1/4 cup salt

Steps

  1. Grill the lamb slices until cooked.
  2. Toast the pita bread.
  3. Layer the pita bread, lamb slices, tomato sauce, and yogurt.
  4. Melt the butter and olive oil together.
  5. Pour the butter and olive oil mixture over the dish.
  6. Sprinkle parsley, sumac, red pepper flakes, and salt on top.
